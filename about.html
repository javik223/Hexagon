ABOUT

The Hexagon Network

A network of quality products and customer oriented services, with focus on meeting the diverse needs of our clients

“Hexagon Network Limited”, is a conglomerate of companies in the hospitality/entertainment/event management; beverage distributorship; Petrochemical dealership, real estate management and contract execution. It has one of the best entertainment, event and real estate management outfits, in southern Nigeria.

The Hexagon Place the relaxation outfit, officially opened to the public in 2002, was created to provide an environment where professionals, businessmen, visitors and fun lovers could get the best hospitality, in a relaxed and secured environment.

Nine years ago, The Hexagon Network Limited moved beyond its initial idea and delved into the world of beverages, real estate management, construction and petrochemicals.

The construction enterprise - Templewood Limited, has accomplished various projects for NDDC, The Edo State Government, The Nigeria Police Force and other corporate bodies and government parastatals.

 

 

THE FOUNDER

Prince Omoregbe Erediauwa / MD/CEO

Entrepreneur and investor. Prince Omoregbe Erediauwa is the founder of Hexagon Network Limited.

With a first degree in Accounting from the University of Benin in Nigeria and an MBA from University of East London United Kingdom, Prince Omoregbe Erediauwa has had passion for high profile spots and artistes in the entertainment world. His passion for such a place led him into early partnership in entertainment with the establishment of an outfit called Woodpecker between 1993 and 1995.

Prince Omoregbe has over the years grown the Hexagon Network Limited, a company he started in 1993 as an undergraduate, into one of the fastest emerging conglomerate in Nigeria.
