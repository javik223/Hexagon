Urban Royale Magazine

 

Urban Royale is the media and publishing arm of The Hexagon Network with primary focus on entertainment, art, culture and tourism.

Urban Royale Magazine, is a 120 pages of trends, eccentric, entertainment magazine that discusses current events, music, film, fashion, lifestyles, poetry, and art. It showcases the hidden talents of Nigerians primarily in the arts and exceptional talents in other endeavours. We take our readers on a journey of fun, discovery and enlightenment.

Our audience includes both the young generation and lifestyle conscious adults. It features expositions on some unknown and unique cultural activities of the various peoples in Nigeria, A tour of popular tourist destinations and events happening around the country.

 

The Maiden Issue

The first edition of the magazine featured the beautiful and irrepressible Stella Damasus speak on her music, movies and talkshow, IBK Spaceship Boy and an interview with His Royal Highness, The Oba of Benin. It also had informative pieces on fashion, beauty, luxury and entertainment plus an city guide piece on Port Harcourt.

Second Issue

The second edition featured rising stars: Yvonne Vixen and Oreka Godis. It also featured an interview with Osaze Odemwingie, and a special piece on First Love Dilemmas, The Oba’s 33 Coronation celebrations, Arise Fashion Week a city guide of Asaba and other entertaining sections

Third Issue

The third edition featured the vintage father and son – Justus Esiri and Dr. Sid. It included an interview with Sam Dede, an on-the-spot piece with Karen Young and a City Guide of Uyo.

With over 10,000 copies in high quality print and production,

Urban Royale magazine has remained a trendsetter and an interesting and educating read for the young generations with a special taste for style and an unquenchable thirst for knowledge of Nigeria, art and culture.
